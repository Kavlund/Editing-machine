Google Drive: where finished videos land

You do this Not connected

Sign in once with the Google account that holds your Drive. Finished videos then deliver straight onto your own storage. Nothing to copy or paste.

Connect Google Drive

First time only: set up the sign-in key (about 5 minutes)
  1. In Google Cloud → APIs & Services → Credentials, create an OAuth client ID of type Web application. Enable the Google Drive API.
  2. Add this exact Authorized redirect URI:
    loading…
  3. In Railway → Variables, add GOOGLE_OAUTH_CLIENT_ID and GOOGLE_OAUTH_CLIENT_SECRET. Then click Connect above.

Delivers onto your own Drive. Adding a client auto-creates their <Client>/Edited/ folder.

2

Notion: link videos to your CPS cards (optional)

You do this Not connected
  1. Create a Notion internal integration and copy its token.
  2. Share your CPS database with that integration, and copy the database ID.
  3. In your Railway → Variables, add: NOTION_API_KEY, NOTION_CPS_DATABASE_ID, NOTION_DELIVERY_MODE=drive_link.
3

Slack: get pinged when a cut is ready (optional)

You do this Not connected
  1. At api.slack.com/apps: Create New App → From scratch, pick your workspace.
  2. Open Incoming Webhooks → On → Add New Webhook to Workspace, choose a channel, Allow.
  3. Copy the webhook URL. In your Railway → Variables, add SLACK_WEBHOOK_URL.

You'll get: "✅ Jordan Blake — 'Morning Routine' is done. Go have a look." with the link.
